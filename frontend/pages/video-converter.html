Video Conversion Tips

Device Compatibility

MP4 with H.264 is compatible with almost all devices including smartphones, tablets, and TVs.

File Size vs Quality

Higher bitrate means better quality but larger files. For web use, 2-5 Mbps is usually sufficient.

Format Selection

MP4 for general use, MOV for Apple devices, WEBM for web embedding, GIF for short animations.

Faster Conversion

Lower resolution and bitrate settings will convert faster but may reduce video quality.
